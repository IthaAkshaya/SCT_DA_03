# HR Employee Attrition Dashboard

## Objective
Interactive Power BI dashboard to answer: **Why are employees leaving?**

## Steps
- Imported HR dataset
- Created AgeGroup column
- Built measures: Attrition Count, Attrition %
- Designed charts by AgeGroup, JobRole, Department, MonthlyIncome
- Added slicers for Attrition, AgeGroup, JobRole, Gender, Department, Region

## Findings
- Employees aged 20–30 have the highest attrition
- Sales Executives and Laboratory Technicians leave more
- Sales Department shows higher attrition
- Lower‑income employees are leaving more

## Conclusion
Attrition is driven by younger age groups, specific job roles, department stress, and salary dissatisfaction.
